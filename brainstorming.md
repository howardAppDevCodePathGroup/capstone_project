# Brainstorming: Unit 7 Capstone Project

## 🧠 Step 1: Initial App Ideas
1.  **Ghost Mode Gym**: An app where you can work out “anonymously” with others in real time. You see silhouettes (no faces, no judgment), their reps, and progress bars. Think: social motivation without the social anxiety.
2.  **MoodMenu**: You tell the app how you feel, and it suggests meals based on your mood. Stressed? Warm comfort foods. Energized? Light, high-protein options. It’s like a therapist… but edible.
3.  **Study Sprint Arena**: Gamifies studying like a competitive sport. You join timed “arenas” where students race to complete tasks. Leaderboards, streaks, and power-ups (like focus boosts or distraction blockers).
4.  **MemoryMap**: Drop memories on a map tied to real locations. Photos, voice notes, or short text entries appear as glowing pins. Revisit places and relive moments like a digital time capsule.
5.  **SkillSwap**: A platform for local users to trade skills or lessons for an hour (e.g., teaching someone SwiftUI in exchange for a cooking lesson).
6.  **Echoes of Lytheria Tracker**: A companion app for a fantasy game world that allows players to track lore, character progress, and elemental power growth.

## ⚖️ Step 2: Top 3 Evaluation

### 1. Journal Puzzle (Final Idea)
* **Mobile**: Uniquely mobile through the use of push notifications for journaling reminders, photo gallery access to save generated art, and real-time social sharing features.
* **Story**: A compelling collaborative experience that turns individual daily reflection into a shared visual reward. It promotes mindfulness and group bonding.
* **Market**: Large potential for friend groups, families, or mental health support groups. Highly unique niche combining AI art with wellness.
* **Habit**: Very habit-forming. Users must contribute their journal entry daily to unlock and complete the group’s image puzzle.
* **Scope**: Well-defined MVP focusing on text input, API integration for image generation, and a partitioned image display.

### 2. Study Sprint Arena
* **Mobile**: Uses real-time notifications and haptic feedback for timers and competitive updates.
* **Story**: Clear value for students needing peer accountability. Peers would respond well to the competitive "arena" aspect.
* **Market**: Massive market within high schools and universities.
* **Habit**: Users would return daily to maintain streaks and climb the leaderboard.
* **Scope**: High technical challenge involving real-time database synchronization for multiple users in an arena.

### 3. Ghost Mode Gym
* **Mobile**: Uses motion sensors to track reps and real-time data streaming for live "ghost" silhouettes.
* **Story**: Solves "gym-timidation" by providing the energy of a group class without the fear of being watched.
* **Market**: Fitness enthusiasts and beginners with social anxiety.
* **Habit**: Encourages a consistent daily workout routine through social motivation.
* **Scope**: Technically complex due to the real-time rendering and sensor-based movement tracking.

## ✅ Final Decision
**The Journal Puzzle App**
We plan to create an app that creates an image from x people's journal entries and creates an AI image based on these x separate entries. The image is partitioned into x amount of pieces and one piece of the image is given to each group member. Members must bring them together to build the full AI image.
